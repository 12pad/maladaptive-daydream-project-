What Is Maladaptive Daydreaming

Maladaptive Daydreaming, (MD) for short is " a condition in which a person daydreams more than he should do, often lasting for hours. This condition is mainly done for relief ".

This definition is from the Long Man Dictionary

Maladaptive daydreaming is an overdose of daydreaming, that is you over dream.

Unlike daydreaming which is fueled by creativity or desire to solve a problem or create something, maladaptive daydreaming is done for reality escape

This condition may at first seem harmless or even helpful, but it becomes a trap in the long run

Maladaptive daydreaming, according to experts estimated that it has affected about 40% of the global population
